Bleeckie Streetie

Children's Television/Media Personality

Lonestar-Emmy nominated Bleeckie Streetie is a puppet most commonly known from YouTube, and from Dallas, TX. Her character is a bubbly 5-year-old journalist who is enthusiastic about life. Her videos primarily focus on interviews with local talents, celebrities, and anybody in between with the purpose of sharing their stories with the world.

Bisby & Magic Hoodie

Picture Book

"The first night Bisby wore her new hoodie to bed, she didn’t see one scaly, red-eyed under-bed monster slink or slither, or creep or crawl. From then on, Bisby knew her hoodie was magical, so she named him 'Magic Hoodie.'"

Harmony's Tunes

Animated Children's Television Show

Harmony's Tunes is an animated television program targeting 2-to-7-year-old children that strives to improve their music literacy by developing their ability to read, write, listen to, and appreciate music, and their understanding of musical terms, instruments, and styles through the use of interactive and entertaining digital content.

  • Date: June 2019

Leslie Rose

Creative Producer

Leslie is a former social media manager for the number one kids’ music brand and currently works for a children's computer science organization. Her passion has always been educating and inspiring children through media, and she received her Master’s in Children’s Media from NYU. While living in New York City, she had the privelege of studying with children’s media industry leaders, interning and working at companies like Nickelodeon and Sesame Workshop, and even getting to be a balloon handler in the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. If she isn't busy creating something super cool for kids, she's most likely singing Broadway musicals to her 75 pound lap dog.

Alan Rose

Executive Producer

Alan is a seasoned professional in the digital media space. While working for a Major League Baseball team for over a decade he generated stories, and filmed, edited, and produced over 3,500 videos from pre-production through broadcast. His content has been broadcasted and distributed locally, nationally, and internationally to a total audience of approximately 20 million fans. His experience includes managing content delivery and collaborating with company executives across multiple departments to develop and execute cross-platform content strategies. Some of his passions include spending time with family, photography, impromptu dancing, and singing horribly off-key!

About Us

glittery & gluey all the way throughy

Glitter & Glue Entertainment is an American digital media production company specializing in children’s programming, both broadcast and digital. Founded by Alan and Leslie Rose in 2010, Glitter & Glue Entertainment creates unique and engaging content distributed across multiple platforms. Starting with videos starring an inquisitive, spunky, five-year-old journalist puppet named Bleeckie, the Glitter & Glue Entertainment brand has expanded into the literary world and is currently in the early stages of pitching their first broadcast television program aimed at children from ages 2-to-7 years old.
